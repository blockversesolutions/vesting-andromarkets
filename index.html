Vesting

Frequently Asked Question

What is vesting?

Vesting is the process of locking tokens for a set time before they can be claimed. For ECM tokens, this ensures stability and long-term commitment from holders.

How does the ECM vesting work?

All ECM tokens are locked for 6 months. After the cliff ends, 100% of your tokens are unlocked at once and can be claimed in full.

What is the cliff period?

The cliff is a 6-month lock period during which no tokens can be accessed or claimed.

Can I claim my tokens before the cliff ends?

No. Tokens remain fully locked during the 6 months. You can only claim them once the cliff period is completed.

What happens after the 6-month cliff?

At the end of 6 months, all your vested ECM tokens are released immediately and can be claimed through the AndroMarkets Vesting Portal.

What if I miss claiming after 6 months?

Your tokens will remain safe in the vesting portal. You can claim them anytime after the 6 months are completed.

Where can I track my vesting status?

You can log in to the AndroMarkets Vesting Portal at any time to view your cliff countdown, allocation, and claim status.
